Favorite Foods

Here I have listed several of my favorite foods, as well as why I like them, & whether they're apetizers, entrees, desserts, or snacks.

Pizza

entree

Bread & cheese are some of my favorite kinds of foods, & they go pretty well together, & when you add toppings, especialy spicy ones the flavor is completed in a way that's hard to describe.

Pasta

entree

Pasta is an incredibly basic but reasonably tasty food that is very versatile as it goes well with most other foods. Personally salt, pepper, cheese, & butter are among my favorite things to put on pasta. It's pretty hard to pick just one type of pasta but if I had to angel hair spaghetti, the texture is immaculate

Pickles

snack

Look I promise they don't all start with P. But yeah, I quite enjoy pickles, they may not be your thing but I like the sour taste, & spicy ones are pretty good too. & some will find this gross but I like the pickle juice too

Chili

entree

When not laced with whatever that sweet sauce is it's basically hamburgers but instead of a couple of hamburgers it's a bunch of hamburger. & spicy. Yum.

Italian Ice

dessert

It was pretty tough to pick between ice cream, gelato, sorbet, & italian ice but I decided on italian ice for reasons I can't really explain.
